About

Jamie Min is a visual designer based in Melbourne, Australia. He specialises in creating visual media in digital formats such as web, UI and 3D.

In addition to the specialised areas, he also has experience in graphic design, branding, 2D motion, illustration and web development (front-end).

Having the diverse range of experience enables him to extensively analyse design problems, comprehensively strategise the design solutions and execute aesthetically engaging visual design productions.
