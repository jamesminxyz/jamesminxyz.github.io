Enhancing the brand with motion

Promoting the new Selz Expert Service with engaging motion design

The process

Storyboard & Animatic

As the new service can cover a broad spectrum of audience, I chose to translate the story into a visual metaphor that represents concepts and ideas.

This way, instead of depicting one specific example case, I believed it could establish a higher level of generality which can keep the audience from taking their interpretation of the story too narrowly.

This direction led to illustrating the scenes in an abstract way using simple shapes which combined together speak for the elements of the story.

Style frames

The brand purple with hue variations is used as a base colour throughout, in order to represent the Selz brand and build a strong visual impression and confidence.

The secondary green colour is used sparingly to trigger a subtle visual interest without conflicting too much with the main purple.

During the animation stage, subtle textures were added in order to complement a better visual depth.
