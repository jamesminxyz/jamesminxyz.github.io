The makeover

The primary element of the rebrand was the logo. With the complete makeover, the new logo aims to give a more modern look and feel.

Logomark

The logomark, representing the letter ’S’, is constructed in symmetry in order to give a sense of stability, security, harmony and balance.

The shape represents the concept of "exchange" or continuous flow of buying and selling (commerce).

Colours

The new colours are small adjustments from the previous, in order to maintain a certain level of continuation of previous Selz.

The new colour scheme has more saturation, which emphasizes the energy of the company’s new direction.

Main colours

The new colour palette that defines the new Selz visual identity.

Additional colours to support the main colours.

The transformation

At the core of the project was the reposition of the business strategy. This led to an overhaul of how the company communicates its new direction with the customers visually on the web.

New feature images

A wide variety of illustrative graphics was created in order to help customers understand the diverse and powerful features of the ecommerce platform.

New feature pages

The new features pages were built in 3 tiers, dividing the huge amount of feature sets into easily digestible small pieces of content.

Market with landing pages

The new landing pages use a simple layout with a big hero image that can be stylistically adapted to target different types of ecommerce merchants.

Enhancing the visual experience

A new illustration style was developed to enhance the experience of the new branding, both internally within the company and externally with the customers.
