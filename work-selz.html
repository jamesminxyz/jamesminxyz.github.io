Selz took a strategic pivot in its business bringing new features and changing its target customers. This pivot entailed a massive transformation of its visual identity and how the company communicates with the customers visually.

The makeover

The primary element of the brand pivot was the logo. With a complete makeover, the new logo aims to give a more modern look and feel.

Logomark

The logomark, representing the letter ’S’, is constructed in symmetry in order to give a sense of stability, security, harmony and balance.

The shape represents an exchange or a continuous flow of buying and selling, which is the essence of commerce.

Colours

The new colour palette is a slight adjustment from the previous palette, in order to maintain a certain level of continuation of previous Selz.

The new palette has more saturation than the previous, which emphasizes the energy of the company’s new direction.

Main colours

The colour palette that defines the new Selz identity.

The transformative momentum

At the core of the project was the reposition of the business strategy. This led to an overhaul of how the company communicates its new direction with the customers visually.

New feature images

A wide variety of illustrative graphics was created in order to help customers understand the diverse and powerful features of the Selz e-commerce platform.

New feature pages

The new features pages were built in 3 tiers, dividing the huge amount of feature sets into easily digestible small pieces of content.

Market with landing pages

The new landing pages use a simple layout with a big hero image that can be stylistically adapted to target different types of ecommerce merchants.

Enhancing the visual experience

A new illustration style was developed to enhance the experience of the new branding, both internally within the company and externally with the customers.
